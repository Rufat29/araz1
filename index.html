Vision

To be Earth's most environmentoriented company, where the customers meet their wood based needs without cutting down trees and increasing the amount of plastic wastes

Mission

From the beginning, our mission has been to provide sustainable alternative to wood industry which are both affordable and quality. Today's world needs greener solutions more than ever, and it's where our mission comes to play. We believe that wood-plastic composites can be game changer for both industry and recycling sector under our management

Sustainability

Social

Social aspect of sustainability has carefully been taken into account in "Polysolutions" company such as maintaining balance between genders of employees, forbidding any kind of discrimination, and offering high standarts to the workers. Administration often organizes parties for encouraging the employees of company to socialize and widen their network.

Environmental

The positive environmental impacts of Polystyrene recycle are decreasing the plastic waste over the world, minimizing ocean pollution, and preventing animal poisoning which are causes by eating plastic waste. In addition to this, as polystyrene is rather light material and takes large space, by recycling it more places are being emptied in landfills.

Economic

One of the main criteria that should be considered while building a business is economic sustainability. In view of the fact that construction sector cannot be stopped as long as humankind exists, there will always be need for product of our company. By providing high quality and economically cheaper commodity to the society, economic sustainability is achieved
